Local School Launches New Coding Club

By Areeba Alam - October 15,2025

ABES City: The students of Greenfield High School have started a new coding club to promote programming skills and teamwork among young learners. The club was inaugurated on Monday by the school principal, Mrs. Sharma.

Encouraging innovations

The club aims to provide students with hands-on experience in coding languages such as Python, C++ and JavaScript. Regular workshops and competitions will be organized to help members learn problem-solving and creative thinking.

Student Participation

Over 50 students from different grades have already joined the club."We want to make learning coding fun and accessible for everyone,"said club president Rohan Mehta.

Future Plans

In the coming months, the club plans to collaborate with local tech experts to conduct guest lectures and mini hackathons. The school management has also promised to provide additional computers and software tools for the initiative.
